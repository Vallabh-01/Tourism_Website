Shivneri Fort

Famous For:

Shivneri Fort is renowned as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire. It holds immense historical significance and is considered a sacred site by many.

Activities to Do:

Visitors to Shivneri Fort can engage in various activities such as exploring the historic ruins, trekking to the fort's summit, visiting the Shivai Devi Temple dedicated to Goddess Shivai, and enjoying panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Famous Places to Visit:

Some of the notable attractions within Shivneri Fort include the Shivai Devi Temple, the Kadelot Point offering scenic views, and the Ganga-Jamuna Twin Peaks. The fortification walls, bastions, and the birthplace of Shivaji Maharaj are also significant landmarks.

How to Reach:

Shivneri Fort is located near Junnar in the Pune district of Maharashtra. The fort is accessible by road from major cities like Pune and Mumbai. Visitors can reach the fort base by car or public transportation and then hike or drive up to the fort entrance.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Shivneri Fort is during the winter months from October to February when the weather is pleasant and suitable for outdoor activities. Avoid visiting during the monsoon season (June to September) as the trekking trails may become slippery and unsafe due to heavy rainfall.

Entry Fee:

There is no entry fee to visit Shivneri Fort. However, visitors may need to pay for parking facilities at the fort base. Donations are appreciated for the maintenance and preservation of the fort.

Accommodation:

While there are no accommodation options available within Shivneri Fort, visitors can find various hotels, resorts, and guesthouses in Junnar town. Junnar offers a range of accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets, making it convenient for travelers to stay while exploring the fort and nearby attractions.
