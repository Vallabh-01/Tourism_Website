Misal Pav

Description:

A popular spicy curry made of sprouted moth beans, topped with farsan, onions, lemon, and coriander, served with pav.

Ingredients:

Sprouted moth beans, onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, spices, farsan, pav bread.

Preparation:

The sprouted beans are cooked with onions, tomatoes, and spices to make a curry. It is topped with farsan and served with pav bread.

Best Places to Try:

Misal joints in Pune, such as Bedekar Misal and Katakirr Misal.

History:

Misal Pav is a traditional Maharashtrian dish, originally from Pune. It has many regional variations, each with its unique spice blend.

Nutritional Information:

Misal Pav is rich in protein and fiber from the sprouts, but can also be high in fat due to the farsan and pav.

Variations:

Variations include Kolhapuri Misal (spicier version), Puneri Misal (milder version), and Dahi Misal (with yogurt).
