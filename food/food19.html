Chicken Kolhapuri

Description:

Chicken Kolhapuri is a spicy chicken curry from Kolhapur, rich in aromatic spices and known for its bold taste.

Ingredients:

Chicken, onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, Kolhapuri masala, coconut, red chili powder, spices.

Preparation:

The chicken is marinated in spices, then cooked with onions, tomatoes, and a special Kolhapuri masala until tender and flavorful.

Best Places to Try:

Local Kolhapuri restaurants and homes, as well as specialty Indian restaurants.

History:

Chicken Kolhapuri originates from the city of Kolhapur in Maharashtra, known for its rich and spicy cuisine.

Nutritional Information:

This dish is high in protein from the chicken and contains various vitamins and minerals from the spices and vegetables.

Variations:

Variations include adjusting the spice levels or using different cuts of chicken to suit personal preferences.
