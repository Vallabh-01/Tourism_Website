Puri Bhaji

Description:

Puri Bhaji consists of deep-fried bread (puri) served with a spiced potato curry (bhaji).

Ingredients:

Wheat flour, potatoes, onions, turmeric, mustard seeds, green chilies.

Preparation:

The puris are made from wheat flour dough, rolled out and deep-fried until puffed and golden. The bhaji is prepared by cooking potatoes with onions, turmeric, mustard seeds, and green chilies.

Best Places to Try:

Local eateries and street vendors in Maharashtra, especially during breakfast hours.

History:

Puri Bhaji has been a staple breakfast dish in Maharashtra for generations, enjoyed for its simplicity and comfort.

Nutritional Information:

Puri Bhaji is rich in carbohydrates and fats. The puris provide a significant amount of calories, while the bhaji offers vitamins and minerals from the potatoes and spices.

Variations:

Variations of Bhaji include adding peas or other vegetables. Some versions of puris are made with whole wheat flour or a mix of flours for a different texture.
