Kolhapuri Mutton

Description:

Kolhapuri Mutton is a spicy and flavorful mutton curry from Kolhapur, known for its rich taste and fiery spice.

Ingredients:

Mutton, onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, red chilies, Kolhapuri masala, coconut.

Preparation:

The mutton is marinated with spices and cooked with a mixture of onions, tomatoes, and Kolhapuri masala until tender. Coconut is added for richness.

Best Places to Try:

Restaurants in Kolhapur and traditional Maharashtrian eateries across the state.

History:

Kolhapuri Mutton has its origins in the city of Kolhapur, known for its bold and spicy cuisine. It is a staple at local celebrations and festivals.

Nutritional Information:

Kolhapuri Mutton is high in protein and fats, providing substantial energy. The spices offer antioxidants and aid in digestion.

Variations:

Variations include adjusting the spice level and adding different vegetables like potatoes or peas. Some recipes use a pressure cooker for faster cooking.
