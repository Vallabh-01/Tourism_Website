Daulatabad Fort

Famous For:

Daulatabad Fort, also known as Devagiri, is famous for its robust defenses and strategic location near Aurangabad in Maharashtra. It holds historical significance as the capital of the Tughlaq dynasty during the medieval period and served as an impregnable stronghold against various invaders.

Activities to Do:

Visitors to Daulatabad Fort can engage in activities such as exploring the fort's massive walls, ramparts, and gateways, climbing up to the Chand Minar for panoramic views, and discovering the ancient architectural marvels within the fort complex. Additionally, photography enthusiasts can capture stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Famous Places to Visit:

Some of the notable attractions within Daulatabad Fort include the Chand Minar (Tower of the Moon), the Chini Mahal (Palace of Mirrors), the Mughal cannon, and the colossal Chandrayan Dwar (Chandrayan Gate). Visitors can also explore the secret passages, underground tunnels, and historical artifacts within the fort premises.

How to Reach:

Daulatabad Fort is located approximately 15 kilometers northwest of Aurangabad city in Maharashtra. The fort is easily accessible by road from Aurangabad, and visitors can hire taxis or use private vehicles to reach the fort entrance. Aurangabad Railway Station is the nearest railhead, and Aurangabad Airport offers domestic flight services.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Daulatabad Fort is during the winter months from October to February when the weather is cool and pleasant for exploring outdoor attractions. Avoid visiting during the summer months (March to June) as temperatures can soar, making it uncomfortable for sightseeing.

Entry Fee:

There is an entry fee to visit Daulatabad Fort, which may vary for Indian and foreign tourists. Additionally, visitors may need to pay for camera fees and guided tours within the fort complex. It is advisable to check the current entry fee and timings before planning your visit.

Accommodation:

While there are no accommodation options available within Daulatabad Fort, visitors can find various hotels, resorts, and guesthouses in Aurangabad city. Aurangabad offers a range of accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets, making it convenient for travelers to stay while exploring the fort and nearby attractions.
