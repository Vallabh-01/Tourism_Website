Shrikhand

Description:

Shrikhand is a sweet dish made from strained yogurt, sugar, and flavored with cardamom and saffron, often garnished with dry fruits.

Ingredients:

Yogurt, sugar, cardamom, saffron, dry fruits.

Preparation:

Yogurt is strained to remove whey, then mixed with sugar, cardamom, and saffron until smooth and creamy. It is chilled and garnished with dry fruits before serving.

Best Places to Try:

Sweets shops and restaurants across Maharashtra, particularly in the cities of Pune and Mumbai.

History:

Shrikhand has been a part of Maharashtrian cuisine for centuries and is often served during festivals and special occasions.

Nutritional Information:

Shrikhand is rich in proteins and fats from yogurt, and sugars provide a quick source of energy. It also contains calcium and probiotics.

Variations:

Variations include adding fruits like mango (Amrakhand) or using different flavorings like rose water or nutmeg.
