Puran Poli

Description:

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread stuffed with a mixture of chana dal, jaggery, and cardamom, typically prepared during festivals.

Ingredients:

Chana dal, jaggery, cardamom, wheat flour, ghee.

Preparation:

The chana dal is cooked and mashed, then mixed with jaggery and cardamom. This filling is encased in wheat dough, rolled out, and cooked on a griddle with ghee.

Best Places to Try:

Traditional sweet shops in Maharashtra, especially during festive seasons.

History:

Puran Poli has been a part of Maharashtrian cuisine for centuries and is particularly associated with the festival of Holi and Ganesh Chaturthi.

Nutritional Information:

Puran Poli is rich in carbohydrates and proteins from the chana dal, with a significant amount of calories due to the jaggery and ghee.

Variations:

Variations include the use of different types of lentils or sweeteners. Some regions add nutmeg or saffron for additional flavor.
