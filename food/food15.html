Tambda Rassa

Description:

Tambda Rassa is a spicy red mutton curry from Kolhapur, known for its bold flavors and fiery taste.

Ingredients:

Mutton, onions, tomatoes, Kolhapuri masala, ginger-garlic paste, coconut, spices.

Preparation:

The mutton is cooked with a mix of Kolhapuri spices, onions, and tomatoes, creating a rich and spicy red curry. Coconut is often added for extra flavor.

Best Places to Try:

Local eateries and restaurants in Kolhapur, Maharashtra.

History:

Tambda Rassa is a traditional dish from Kolhapur, known for its robust and spicy cuisine.

Nutritional Information:

Tambda Rassa is high in protein and fats. It is also high in calories due to the rich ingredients used.

Variations:

Variations include adjusting the level of spice and using different cuts of meat.
