Pandhra Rassa

Description:

Pandhra Rassa is a mild chicken broth from Kolhapur, flavored with coconut milk and spices, typically served with rice or roti.

Ingredients:

Chicken, coconut milk, garlic, ginger, spices, salt, ghee.

Preparation:

The chicken is cooked in a broth of coconut milk and spices, creating a flavorful and creamy soup. It is typically seasoned with garlic, ginger, and other spices.

Best Places to Try:

Local restaurants and eateries in Kolhapur, Maharashtra.

History:

Pandhra Rassa is a traditional dish from Kolhapur, often enjoyed as part of a larger meal with rice or roti.

Nutritional Information:

Pandhra Rassa is high in protein from the chicken and contains healthy fats from the coconut milk.

Variations:

Variations include using different meats or adding vegetables to the broth.
