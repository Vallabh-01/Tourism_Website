Murud-Janjira Fort

Famous For:

Murud-Janjira Fort is famous for being a formidable sea fort located off the coast of Murud in the Arabian Sea. It holds historical significance as one of the strongest marine forts in India and remained unconquered despite several attempts by foreign invaders, including the Portuguese, British, and Marathas.

Activities to Do:

Visitors to Murud-Janjira Fort can engage in activities such as exploring the fort's massive walls, bastions, and cannons, learning about its history and architectural features, enjoying panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, and experiencing boat rides to reach the fort island. Photography enthusiasts can capture stunning views of the fort and surrounding landscape.

Famous Places to Visit:

Some of the notable attractions within Murud-Janjira Fort include the Palace of the Nawabs, the Janjira Lake, the freshwater well, and the Kasa Fort situated nearby. Visitors can also explore the fort's various gates, secret passages, and architectural marvels while learning about its intriguing history and maritime significance.

How to Reach:

Murud-Janjira Fort is located on an island near Murud village in the Raigad district of Maharashtra. The fort is accessible by boat from Murud Beach, which is the main embarkation point for visiting the fort. Visitors can hire private boats or join organized boat tours from Murud Beach to reach the fort island.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Murud-Janjira Fort is during the winter months from October to February when the weather is pleasant and suitable for exploring outdoor attractions. Avoid visiting during the monsoon season (June to September) as rough seas and inclement weather may disrupt boat services to the fort.

Entry Fee:

There is an entry fee to visit Murud-Janjira Fort, which may vary for Indian and foreign tourists. Additionally, visitors may need to pay for boat rides to reach the fort island from Murud Beach. It is advisable to check the current entry fee and boat charges before planning your visit.

Accommodation:

While there are no accommodation options available within Murud-Janjira Fort, visitors can find various hotels, resorts, and homestays in Murud village. Murud offers a range of accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets, making it convenient for travelers to stay while exploring the fort and nearby attractions.
