Bharit

Description:

Bharit is a dish made from roasted and mashed eggplant mixed with onions, tomatoes, and spices.

Ingredients:

Eggplant (brinjal), onions, tomatoes, green chilies, garlic, mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, coriander, salt, oil.

Preparation:

Eggplants are roasted until charred, then peeled and mashed. Onions, tomatoes, and spices are saut√©ed separately and mixed with the mashed eggplant to form a flavorful dish.

Best Places to Try:

Bharit is a popular homemade dish in Maharashtra, often served with bhakri or chapati.

History:

Bharit has its roots in traditional Maharashtrian cuisine, where eggplant is a commonly used vegetable in various dishes.

Nutritional Information:

Bharit is low in calories and rich in dietary fiber, vitamins, and minerals. It's a healthy addition to a balanced diet.

Variations:

Variations may include adding yogurt or coconut milk for a creamier texture, or incorporating different spices and herbs for flavor variation.
