Bombil Fry

Description:

Bombil Fry is a crispy fried preparation of Bombay duck fish, coated in semolina and spices, popular in coastal Maharashtra.

Ingredients:

Bombay duck fish, semolina, rice flour, spices (turmeric, red chili powder, salt), oil.

Preparation:

The fish is marinated in spices, then coated in a mixture of semolina and rice flour before being fried until crispy.

Best Places to Try:

Coastal seafood restaurants and street food stalls in Maharashtra.

History:

Bombil Fry is a traditional dish from the coastal regions of Maharashtra, where Bombay duck fish is commonly found.

Nutritional Information:

This dish is high in protein from the fish and provides healthy fats from the frying oil.

Variations:

Variations can include different types of coatings or spices to enhance the flavor.
