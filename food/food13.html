Thecha

Description:

Thecha is a spicy chutney made from green chilies, garlic, and peanuts, often served with bhakri.

Ingredients:

Green chilies, garlic, peanuts, salt, oil.

Preparation:

The chilies and garlic are roasted and then ground together with peanuts and salt to form a coarse paste. It can be served raw or saut√©ed in oil for added flavor.

Best Places to Try:

Traditional households and local eateries in Maharashtra.

History:

Thecha is a traditional condiment in Maharashtrian cuisine, often prepared in rural areas and served with rustic dishes like bhakri.

Nutritional Information:

Thecha is low in calories but high in spice. It provides some protein and healthy fats from the peanuts.

Variations:

Variations include using red chilies instead of green, adding coconut, or incorporating other spices for different flavors.
