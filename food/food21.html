Kolhapuri Misal

Description:

Kolhapuri Misal is a spicy, tangy dish made of sprouted beans, topped with farsan, onions, and served with pav (bread rolls).

Ingredients:

Sprouted beans, onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, Kolhapuri masala, farsan, pav, coriander.

Preparation:

The sprouted beans are cooked in a spicy gravy with onions, tomatoes, and Kolhapuri masala, then topped with farsan and served with pav.

Best Places to Try:

Street food stalls and traditional eateries in Kolhapur and other parts of Maharashtra.

History:

Kolhapuri Misal is a popular breakfast dish from Kolhapur, known for its fiery and flavorful taste.

Nutritional Information:

This dish is rich in protein from the sprouts and provides a mix of carbohydrates and fiber.

Variations:

Variations include different levels of spiciness or additional toppings like yogurt or lemon juice.
