Padmadurg Fort

Famous For:

Padmadurg Fort is famous for being a key naval fort built by the Maratha ruler Shivaji Maharaj to strengthen his maritime defenses against foreign invasions. The fort served as a strategic outpost to protect the coastline and safeguard trade routes along the Arabian Sea.

Activities to Do:

Visitors to Padmadurg Fort can engage in activities such as exploring the fort's ramparts, bastions, and citadel, witnessing the remains of ancient structures, and learning about its naval significance. Additionally, visitors can enjoy scenic views of the Arabian Sea, indulge in photography, and relax amidst the tranquil surroundings.

Famous Places to Visit:

Some of the notable attractions within Padmadurg Fort include the main entrance gate, the bastions overlooking the sea, and the remnants of fortifications and cannons. The fort's strategic location offers panoramic views of the coastline and nearby islands, making it a popular destination for history enthusiasts and nature lovers.

How to Reach:

Padmadurg Fort is situated off the coast of Murud in the Arabian Sea, making it accessible only by boat or ferry. Visitors can reach the fort by hiring local boats from the coastal villages of Murud or Rajapuri. Alternatively, organized boat tours and excursions are available from nearby tourist destinations.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Padmadurg Fort is during the winter and post-monsoon months from October to March when the weather is pleasant and suitable for boat rides and exploration. It is advisable to avoid visiting during the monsoon season (June to September) due to rough seas and limited boat services.

Entry Fee:

There may be an entry fee or charges for boat rides to visit Padmadurg Fort, which may vary depending on the mode of transportation and tour packages. Visitors are advised to inquire about entry fees, boat availability, and safety precautions before planning their visit to the fort.

Accommodation:

Since Padmadurg Fort is an offshore fortification, there are no accommodation options available on the fort itself. However, visitors can find various hotels, resorts, and homestays in the nearby coastal towns of Murud and Dapoli. These towns offer a range of accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets.
