Torna Fort

Famous For:

Torna Fort is famous for being the first fort captured by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It holds historical significance as one of the key forts in the Maratha Empire and played a crucial role in Shivaji's military campaigns.

Activities to Do:

Visitors to Torna Fort can engage in various activities such as trekking, exploring the fort's ancient ruins and bastions, enjoying panoramic views of the Sahyadri mountain range, and experiencing the rich history and culture of the Marathas.

Famous Places to Visit:

Some of the notable attractions within Torna Fort include the Bini Darwaja (main entrance), the Menghai Goddess Temple, the Kothi Darwaja, and the Budhla Machi offering stunning views of the surrounding valleys. The fortification walls and bastions are also significant landmarks.

How to Reach:

Torna Fort is located near Velhe in the Pune district of Maharashtra. The fort is accessible by road from major cities like Pune and Mumbai. Visitors can reach the fort base by car or public transportation and then hike or drive up to the fort entrance.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Torna Fort is during the winter months from October to February when the weather is pleasant and suitable for outdoor activities. Avoid visiting during the monsoon season (June to September) as the trekking trails may become slippery and unsafe due to heavy rainfall.

Entry Fee:

There is no entry fee to visit Torna Fort. However, visitors may need to pay for parking facilities at the fort base. Donations are appreciated for the maintenance and preservation of the fort.

Accommodation:

While there are no accommodation options available within Torna Fort, visitors can find various hotels, resorts, and guesthouses in Velhe town. Velhe offers a range of accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets, making it convenient for travelers to stay while exploring the fort and nearby attractions.
