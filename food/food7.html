Sol Kadhi

Description:

Sol Kadhi is a refreshing drink made from kokum and coconut milk, often enjoyed with rice or as a soothing beverage after meals.

Ingredients:

Kokum, coconut milk, garlic, green chilies, coriander.

Preparation:

Kokum is soaked in water to extract its flavor, then mixed with coconut milk, garlic, and green chilies. It is garnished with coriander before serving.

Best Places to Try:

Coastal Maharashtra, especially in Konkan region restaurants.

History:

Sol Kadhi has its roots in the coastal Konkan region of Maharashtra. It is traditionally consumed as a digestive aid and to cool the body.

Nutritional Information:

Sol Kadhi is low in calories and rich in electrolytes. It is also known for its digestive properties.

Variations:

Variations include adding cumin or mustard seeds for extra flavor. Some versions are made spicier by adding more green chilies.
